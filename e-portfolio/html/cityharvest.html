CityHarvest Website

As a way of expressing gratitude, I created the website for CityHarvest Church, which was my family away from home during my first year of study. The website provides:

  • Easy access to service schedules and event updates
  • Contact information for church members and visitors

This project allowed me to combine my technical skills with my desire to give back to a community that supported me during a crucial time in my life.
