CAPSTONE PROJECT - Document Sharing and CV Generator App

This website was created as part of our capstone project for 3rd-year Computer Science. I worked collaboratively with some of my classmates as a team to design and develop the platform. Together, we focused on solving real-world problems by combining our skills in programming, problem-solving, and teamwork.

Capstone Project Demo

Watch this short demo to see how the document-sharing and CV generation app works.

Features:

Share Documents with QR Codes:

  • Upload a document, and the app creates a unique QR code for it.
  • Scan the QR code on another device to quickly access and download the document.

Create CVs Automatically:

  • Users can fill in their details, like personal and job-related information.
  • The app generates a professional CV for them automatically.
